FAQs

Answers before you join.

Do I have to apply to each company separately?
No. One assessment per event covers every company hiring in that stream that week — you're considered by all of them at once, from a single profile.
What happens if I'm not matched?
You'll get specific written feedback on your assessment and can register for the next event in your stream — most streams run weekly, so you never wait long.
Is MatchDay really free for jobseekers?
Yes. MatchDay is completely free for jobseekers. Companies pay to take part in events — you just create a profile and show up.
Can I register for more than one stream?
Yes, though each stream has its own assessment, so most jobseekers focus on the one or two that fit them best.
