Pre-evaluated candidate process

Screened before you see them.

Every candidate clears a gated funnel. You only meet the ones who make it through.

STAGE 01

Aptitude & MCQ

Proctored aptitude and role-specific multiple-choice assessment with webcam gating.

STAGE 02

Coding assessment

Hands-on coding evaluation graded on correctness and approach — no take-home guesswork.

STAGE 03

TARA AI interview

Hiringhood's AI interviewer, TARA, assesses communication, depth, and role fit.

MATCH-READY

Ready to interview

Only candidates who clear all three stages enter your recommended pool.
